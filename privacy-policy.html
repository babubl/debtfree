Privacy Policy

DebtFree — AI Debt Stress Analyzer

Last Updated: February 19, 2026 · Developer: Babu Balasubramanian

1. Introduction

DebtFree ("the App") is a personal finance tool designed to help Indian households analyze and manage their debt. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

2. Data Collection

DebtFree does NOT collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data whatsoever.

Specifically, the App:

  • Does NOT collect personal identification information
  • Does NOT collect or transmit financial data
  • Does NOT use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • Does NOT require an internet connection
  • Does NOT access contacts, camera, microphone, or location

3. Local Data Storage

All data you enter is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device, is not accessible to the developer, and is deleted when you uninstall the App or use "Clear All Data" in Settings.

4. No Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data. The App is fully self-contained and operates entirely offline.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since zero data is collected, it is compliant with COPPA and similar regulations.

6. Data Security

No data is transmitted or stored externally. Your financial information remains solely on your device, protected by your device's own security measures.

7. Permissions

The App requests zero device permissions. It does not access internet, storage beyond its sandbox, camera, microphone, location, contacts, or any other device feature.

8. Disclaimer

DebtFree is an informational tool and does not provide financial advice. The Debt Health Score and strategies are mathematical estimates. Please consult a SEBI-registered financial advisor for personalized guidance.
